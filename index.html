Smart Urban Mining Platform

Transforming E-Waste into Valuable Resources

About This Project

Smart Urban Mining Platform is developed to promote responsible e-waste recycling. Electronic waste contains valuable materials such as copper, aluminium and precious metals. This platform encourages people to donate e-waste and helps track environmental impact.
