Recycling Process

Collection

Electronic waste including computers, phones and cables are collected from homes and industries.

Sorting

E-waste is sorted into plastics, circuit boards and metals to increase recycling efficiency.

Dismantling

Devices are dismantled to separate reusable components and hazardous materials.

Metal Recovery

Valuable metals like copper, aluminium and gold are extracted through recycling processes.

Industry Visit – E-Waste Facility

Students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering visited an E-waste recycling facility at MIDC Bhosari.

Experts demonstrated dismantling of devices and recovery of valuable metals.
