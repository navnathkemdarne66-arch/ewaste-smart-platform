Transforming E-Waste into Valuable Resources

Recycling Process

Collection

Electronic waste including computers, phones, cables and circuit boards is collected from households and industries. Proper collection prevents hazardous materials from entering landfills.

Sorting

Collected waste is sorted based on materials such as plastics, circuit boards and copper wiring. Efficient sorting increases metal recovery efficiency.

Dismantling

Devices are dismantled to separate valuable components such as motherboards, processors and power units. Hazardous materials are removed safely.

Metal Recovery

Precious metals such as copper, aluminium, gold and silver are recovered using mechanical separation and metallurgical processes. This is called urban mining.

Industry Visit – E-Waste Recycling Facility

Students of Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering visited an E-waste recycling facility at MIDC Bhosari operated by Electrofine Recycling Pvt Ltd in association with ECA.

During the visit experts demonstrated dismantling of electronic devices and recovery of metals such as copper and aluminium.
