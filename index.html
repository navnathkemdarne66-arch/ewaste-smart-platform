Smart Urban Mining Platform

Transforming E-Waste into Valuable Resources

About This Project

The Smart Urban Mining Platform promotes responsible electronic waste recycling and resource recovery. E-waste contains valuable materials such as copper, aluminium and precious metals which can be recovered through safe recycling processes.

Our Services

E-Waste Collection

Collection of obsolete electronics from homes and industries.

Secure Recycling

Electronic devices are dismantled using safe recycling methods.

Material Recovery

Precious metals are recovered and reused in manufacturing.

Recycling Process

Collection

Electronic waste is gathered from homes and collection centers.

Sorting

Waste is categorized based on material type and device category.

Dismantling

Devices are dismantled to separate valuable components.

Metal Recovery

Metals such as copper, aluminium and gold are extracted.
